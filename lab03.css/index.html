French Toast

Sarah's special one of a kind French Toast

Ingredients:

  • bread
  • eggs
  • butter
  • sugar
  • cinnamon
  • vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Crack open your eggs
  2. Mix your cinnamon and vanilla in it
  3. Dip the bread of your choice in the eggs
  4. Place your dipped bread in a hot pan with butter and fry it on both sides

A favorite dish, french toast is enjoyable for any occasion because it's tasty, simple to prepare, flexible, and nostalgic.
